Everyone is cautious about weight and calorie they take everyday. The project focuses on individual’s daily calorie intake. It will be implemented on Android mobile platform to calculate the sum of calories a person takes daily and to provide suggestions according to users’ profile, such as age, current figure, health status and ideal figures.









Main Functions
  • Record individual’s daily calorie intake
  • Calculate the sum of the calories taken in daily
  • Recommend meals to users
